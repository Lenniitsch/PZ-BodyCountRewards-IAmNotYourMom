[ v1.0.0 — Initial Release ]
I'm not your mom. Add whatever unrealistic, overpowered traits you want.

[b]What this does:[/b] Adds 2 positive and 4 negative vanilla traits
that the base BCR mod excludes. More dangerous, more powerful.

[b]Positive Traits (Earnable):[/b]
[list]
[*] [b]Brave[/b] — Panic reduction. Look a zombie in the eye and yawn.
[*] [b]Desensitized[/b] — Complete panic immunity. You've seen things.
    Nothing fazes you anymore.
[/list]

[b]Negative Traits (Removable):[/b]
[list]
[*] [b]Short Sighted[/b] — Can't see far. Glasses not included.
[*] [b]Hard of Hearing[/b] — What? SPEAK UP.
[*] [b]Insomniac[/b] — Sleep is for the weak. Also incompatible
    with getting restful sleep.
[*] [b]Deaf[/b] — Complete hearing loss. Hope your eyes are good.
[/list]

[b]Sandbox Options:[/b] Each trait has its own enable/disable toggle
under the [b]BodyCountRewardsThirdPartyTraits[/b] sandbox page.
Your mom can turn them off if she's reading over your shoulder.

[b]For Mod Developers:[/b] This addon is also a living code example of
BCR's third-party extensibility API. Check the source — call
BCR.RegisterCustomTraits() with your trait data, add your sandbox
toggles, and you're done. Traits merge automatically into the
reward pools, stats catalog, and history.

[b]Requires BodyCountRewards (BCR)[/b] loaded first.
Safe to remove mid-game — earned traits stay on your character.
