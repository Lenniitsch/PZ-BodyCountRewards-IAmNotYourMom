[ v1.0.0 - Initial Release ]
I'm not your mom. Add whatever unrealistic, overpowered traits you want.

Adds 2 positive and 4 negative vanilla traits that the base BCR mod excludes. More dangerous, more powerful.

POSITIVE TRAITS (Earnable)
- Brave & Desensitized 

NEGATIVE TRAITS (Removable)
- Short Sighted, Hard of Hearing, Insomniac, Deaf

SANDBOX OPTIONS
Each trait has its own enable/disable toggle under a new "AddOn Traits" sandbox page. 

FOR MOD DEVELOPERS
This addon is also a living code example of BCR's third-party extensibility API. Check the source - call BCR.RegisterCustomTraits() with your trait data, add your sandbox toggles, and you're done. Traits merge automatically into the reward pools, stats catalog, and history.

Requires BodyCountRewards (BCR) loaded first.
This mod is safe to remove mid-game, since it only adds more vanilla traits. So earned traits stay on your character. This might differ for custom trait mods.
