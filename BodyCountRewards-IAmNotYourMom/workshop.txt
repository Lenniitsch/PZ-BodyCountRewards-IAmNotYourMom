description=[h2]Added Traits[/h2]
description=[b]Positive (Earnable):[/b]
description=[list]
description=[*] [b]Brave[/b] - Panic reduction. For those who look a zombie in the eye and yawn.
description=[*] [b]Desensitized[/b] - Complete panic immunity. You've seen things. Nothing fazes you anymore. Not even corpse looting.
description=[/list]
description=[b]Negative (Removable):[/b]
description=[list]
description=[*] [b]Short Sighted[/b] - Can't see far. Reading glasses not included.
description=[*] [b]Hard of Hearing[/b] - What? SPEAK UP.
description=[*] [b]Insomniac[/b] - Sleep is for the weak. Also incompatible with getting restful sleep.
description=[*] [b]Deaf[/b] - Complete hearing loss. Hope you have good eyes.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]Sandbox Options[/h2]
description=Each added trait has its own enable/disable toggle in the [b]BodyCountRewardsThirdPartyTraits[/b] sandbox page. Toggle them off if your mom is reading.
description=
description=[h2]For Mod Developers[/h2]
description=This addon is a living code example of BCR's extensibility API. Check the source code to see how easy it is to register your own traits:
description=[list]
description=[*] Call [b]BCR.RegisterCustomTraits()[/b] with your trait data
description=[*] Provide your own sandbox toggles with a unique namespace
description=[*] Traits merge automatically into BCR's reward pools, UI catalog, and history
description=[/list]
