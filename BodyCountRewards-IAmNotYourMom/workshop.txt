description=[h2]FAQ[/h2]
description=
description=[b]Q: Why are these traits not in the base BCR mod?[/b]
description=A: The base mod aims for lore-friendly, balanced selections. This addon is for players who want anything goes. I am not your mom. You decide.
description=
description=[b]Q: Can other mods add traits like this?[/b]
description=A: Yes. BCR has a public extensibility API. This addon doubles as a reference implementation. See the For Mod Developers section below.
description=
description=[b]Q: Removed Short Sighted but still can't see far?[/b]
description=A: The trait is removed correctly from your character, but the visual effect (blur/vision range) does not refresh until you reload the game (quit to main menu and load your save). This is a PZ limitation, not a BCR bug.
description=
description=[h2]For Mod Developers[/h2]
description=[b]Download this mod and study the source code.[/b] It is the simplest possible example of integrating custom traits into Body Count Rewards:
description=[list]
description=[*] Call [b]BCR.RegisterCustomTraits()[/b] with your trait data, exclusions, and sandbox namespace
description=[*] Traits merge automatically into BCR's reward pools, UI catalog, and history
description=[*] BCR core provides [b]BCR.RunThirdPartyTests()[/b] to verify all addons at once.
description=[/list]
